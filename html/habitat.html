habitat {MDCv1}R Documentation

Habitat covariates

Description

A data frame of habitat covariates for all available cells in the study area.

Usage

data("habitat")

Format

A data frame with 185 observations on the following 5 variables.

utmX

a numeric vector of X coordinate

utmY

a numeric vector of Y coordinate

unit.id

a numeric vector of cell_IDs

prcnt.sage

a numeric vector of Percent Big-sage

elevation

a numeric vector of elevation (km)

Source

Nielson, R. M., B. F. J. Manly, L. L. McDonald, H. Sawyer, and T. L. McDonald. 2009. Estimating habitat selection when GPS fix success is less than 1. Ecology 90:2956-2962.

Examples

str(habitat)
